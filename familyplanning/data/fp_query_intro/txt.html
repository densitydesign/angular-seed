Harvesting the Relevant Webpages

We designed 9 different queries to collect the most relevant web-pages addressing the Family Planning. The different queries have been designed to address as much as possible “nuances” of the Family Planning issues: from the more medical ones to the more daily ones.
We qualified and tagged all the webpages accordingly to their contents obtaining three main webpages groups:

  • Medical Information;
  • Contentious Information;
  • Natural Family Planning.
